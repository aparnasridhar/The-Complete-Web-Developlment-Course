What do you get with XPP?

Background

  • XPP is an app for solving differential equations, delay equations, functional equations, boundary value problems, and stochastic equations.
  • XPP will draw nullclines, direction fields, and two- and three-dimensional plots of solutions as well as find equilibrium points, their stability and separatrices.
  • It is based on the popular program XPPAUT and uses the same ODE file syntax.

Features

  • Choose from a variety of numerical Integrators.
  • Customize parameters and simulate real time behavior changes.
  • Add any of thousands of equations to your library directly from Dropbox or import via iTunes.

Teaching Support

  • Project on big screens from the iPad/iPhone and use as a teaching aid.
  • Simulate equations real time while teaching courses.
